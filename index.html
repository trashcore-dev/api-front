FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What is Trashcore API?▼
A free REST API providing AI chat, media downloaders, social scrapers, weather, ephoto text effects and more — built for bots, apps and automation.
Is there a rate limit?▼
No. There are no rate limits and no API keys required. Just make requests freely.
What AI models are supported?▼
GPT-4o-mini, o3-mini, Groq Llama 3.3 70B, Pollinations OpenAI, Perplexity (turbo, claude, gpt-4o, sonar), Whisper STT, and TTS with multiple voices.
What languages can I use?▼
Any language that can make HTTP requests — Node.js, Python, PHP, Java, Dart, curl, and more.
How do I contact support?▼
Reach us on WhatsApp (+254 787 909 276) or Telegram (@trashcoredev2). You can also open the menu and tap the contact links directly.
